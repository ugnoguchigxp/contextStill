# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to make this project a respectful and productive space for contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and specific.
- Keep technical criticism focused on code, behavior, documentation, and tradeoffs.
- Assume good intent, but clarify ambiguity early.
- Respect maintainers' scope decisions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests.
- Abusive behavior toward maintainers or contributors.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or delete comments; close issues; reject contributions; or block participants when needed to keep the project usable and safe.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner.
